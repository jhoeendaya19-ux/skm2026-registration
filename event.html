4

FAQ And Contact

Event help content can be updated by the organizer as details are finalized.

Is my registration confirmed after submission?

No. Your registration is pending until the organizer verifies your payment proof.

Can I edit my registration after submitting?

Changes must go through the organizer to protect the accuracy of runner and payment records.

Are minors allowed to register?

Yes. A signed parental consent form is required for participants below 18 years old on race day.
